True value

Instead of a single price for a range of people, we quote you as an individual and offer the most suitable premium.

Control your cover

Choose your cover amount, from R30 000 to R100 000. You can change this cover amount whenever you want.

Real flexibility

Add your spouse, up to 8 children and even your parents or parents-in-law. You can also add or remove family members whenever you want.

Pause and play

If times get tough, pause your cover and premium for up to three months. When you begin paying again, your cover will resume with no waiting periods.

Bonus month

Get a bonus month or premium holiday for every 12 months of premiums we receive. You can ‘bank’ up to three of these bonus months, for when you really need them.
